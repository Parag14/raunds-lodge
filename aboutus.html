About us
Our aim is to provide quality care that meets the individual needs of each resident in a homely and friendly atmosphere whilst at the same time supporting relatives and friends. We are pleased to accept Service Users for long term, short term for convalescence and for holiday stay. When people have worked hard throughout their life, we feel they deserve extra cherishing. At Raunds Lodge individuality is emphasised, with staff that have time to give attention to small detail.

Raunds nursing home has been a family run business since 1995. From 1995 onwards we have strived for excellence and improvement on both the nursing care front as well as the facilities we offer service users.

In 2003 we applied for planning permission and after a few hurdles planning permission was approved in 2006. Work was started in 2006 on the extension to the care home which now has 16 brand new, fully furbished, single en-suite rooms.

The rooms are spacious and have all the facilities that service users may require. Along with the new en-suite bedrooms, a new lounge has been added for service users in the extended building. The new wing also had a lot of input from the Alzheimer society who liaised with us greatly with the lay out and colour scheme of the new wing

We have a new laundry facilities and new disinfection units to maintain our high standards of cleanliness and hygiene which we take very seriously.

Having completed the first phase of the development, we are currently going through the second phase. The second phase will be to completely refurbish the main building, with all the previous double rooms in the entire building being made into single en-suite rooms.. This will give our service users a more spacious environment and also provide a sense of privacy that they might require when having visitors. We expect the second phase to be complete by June end.

We believe in residents being able to enjoy all facilities that we offer. Our spacious garden for the residents is one which they have full use of through out the year. We actively encourage residents to make full use of the garden, as well as being involved in the gardens preservation. There is also ample Car parking space for visitors with easy access to the building and garden.

At Raunds Lodge we believe in keeping high standards which is why the majority of our care assistants have a minimum NVQ Level 2 in nursing. We have a qualified chef who cooks fresh meals through the day. This ensures that our residents have a healthy balanced diet.

Over all we pride ourselves on our high standards and professional friendly staff. We aim to make the residents time with us as comfortable as we possibly can and we will do everything with in our power to make sure that residents and loved ones feel that we are providing the highest quality of care.
