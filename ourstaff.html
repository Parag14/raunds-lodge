Our Staff
Raunds Lodge Nursing Home will try to provide its service users with a secure, relaxed and homely environment in which their care, well being and comfort are of prime importance.
The management and staff will endeavour to maintain the individuality, independence, dignity and respect those in our care. We will ensure the staff and service users enjoy happy and homely atmosphere at all times.

Details of Staff

The home employs 6 Registered Nurses including 1 Registered Mental Nurse, 9 Nursing assistants ( including Bank staff ). Two have NVQ Level 2. Small proportion of staff is adaptation nurses working as care staff. Two cooks and 3 housekeepers are employed. The staff are selected for their qualities of reliability, integrity, caring nature, skill, friendliness and professionalism. The home requests at least two references and an enhanced CRB check. All new staff are given in-house induction training by a qualified senior staff in the following:

  • Code of conduct
  • Confidentiality
  • The rights of Service users
  • Health & Safety
  • Food hygiene & Safety
  • Personal care tasks
  • Nursing assistants responsibility
  • Use of equipment

All new staff will complete an induction form. The home encourages all Nursing assistants to train to at least NVQ level 2 in care. We have criteria that all new employees undertake NVQ training within six months of commencement. There are various in house training courses provided by external trainers. There is further potential for staff development through the use of training videos in various topics. The staff are also sent to training college for more in depth training. All staff members received a four-day training course in dementia called “ Yesterday Today Tomorrow “ in association with Alzheimers Society approved trainer. We have formed a partnership with this trainer to provide ongoing training and support for our staff.
